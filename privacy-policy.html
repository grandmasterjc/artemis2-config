Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Artemis II Tracker ("the App") is developed by Bit Factory. This policy describes how the App handles your information.

Data We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or login is required
  • No personal information is requested or stored
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are included
  • No cookies or identifiers are used
  • No data is shared with third parties

Network Requests

The App makes network requests to the following public services to display mission data:

  • NASA Open APIs (api.nasa.gov) — Astronomy Picture of the Day, space weather data
  • Launch Library 2 (ll.thespacedevs.com) — Launch date and status
  • NASA AROW (nasa.gov) — Real-time Orion telemetry (when active during the mission)
  • RSS feeds — News from NASA, Spaceflight Now, Ars Technica, NASASpaceflight, Space.com
  • GitHub (raw.githubusercontent.com) — App configuration file

These requests contain no personal information. Only publicly available mission data is retrieved.

Local Storage

The App stores your unit preference (Imperial/Metric) locally on your device using UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.
